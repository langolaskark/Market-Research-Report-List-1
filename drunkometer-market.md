In contrast, Semiconductor Type breathalyzers rely on electronic sensors that detect alcohol vapor, providing quicker results and being more cost-effective. Both technologies serve the essential function of promoting safety by preventing drunk driving through accessible alcohol detection solutions.</p></p>
