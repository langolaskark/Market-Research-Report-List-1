<p><strong>The Brake Spring Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Progressive Spring</li><li>Linear Spring</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The brake spring market is primarily divided into two types: progressive springs and linear springs. Progressive springs provide varying resistance based on compression, enhancing performance through improved handling and comfort. They are often used in applications requiring adaptability to different loads. In contrast, linear springs offer consistent resistance throughout their compression range, making them ideal for applications that require predictable and straightforward performance. The choice between these springs depends on specific application needs and desired performance characteristics in brake systems.</p></p>
