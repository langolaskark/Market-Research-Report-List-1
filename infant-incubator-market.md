<p><p>The infant incubator market is primarily divided into two types: transport infant incubators and normal infant incubators. Transport infant incubators are designed for the movement of critically ill neonates, featuring lightweight, portable designs and built-in monitoring systems to ensure stability during transit. Normal infant incubators, on the other hand, are utilized in hospital settings to provide a controlled environment for premature or vulnerable infants, maintaining optimal temperature and humidity levels while allowing for medical care and observation.</p></p>
