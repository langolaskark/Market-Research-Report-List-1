The APT market comprises three main types: the neutralization method, where ammonium hydroxide neutralizes tungstic acid; the tungsten acid method, which involves direct precipitation from tungsten acid solutions; and the thermal decomposition method, where APT is produced through heating to break down tungsten compounds. Each method influences production cost, purity, and application in various industries.</p></p>
