<p><p>The bulk container packaging market encompasses various types designed for transporting and storing large quantities of goods. Drums are sturdy, cylindrical containers suitable for liquids and chemicals. Pails, often smaller, are used for paint and food products. Material handling containers are designed for easy transport and storage. Bulk container liners are protective barriers within containers, while RIBCs (Reusable Intermediate Bulk Containers) and FIBCs (Flexible Intermediate Bulk Containers) are used for bulk powders and granules. Flexi tanks cater to bulk liquid transportation. Other specialized containers also exist, addressing diverse needs.</p></p>
