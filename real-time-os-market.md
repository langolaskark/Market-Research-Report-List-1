<p><ul><li>Hard Real-time</li><li>Soft Real-time</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The real-time operating system (RTOS) market is primarily categorized into hard real-time and soft real-time systems. Hard real-time systems are critical where missing a deadline can lead to catastrophic failures, commonly used in medical devices and aerospace applications. In contrast, soft real-time systems allow for some flexibility in meeting deadlines, often used in multimedia applications and telecommunications, where performance is important but not life-threatening. Both types address the unique timing and reliability needs of their respective applications.</p></p>
