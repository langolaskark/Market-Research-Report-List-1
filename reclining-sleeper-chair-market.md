<p><p>The reclining sleeper chair market is categorized into three main types: manual, electrical, and pneumatic. Manual reclining chairs require users to physically pull a lever or push back to adjust the position, offering simplicity and a lower cost. Electrical models utilize motors to facilitate easy adjustments at the push of a button, providing enhanced comfort and convenience. Pneumatic chairs use compressed air systems for smooth recline and adjustability, allowing for precise customization of seating positions and greater user control.</p></p>
