<p><ul><li>Unipolar</li><li>Multipolar</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The implantable pulse generator (IPG) market is segmented into unipolar and multipolar types. Unipolar IPGs deliver electrical impulses through a single electrode, offering simplicity and efficiency, and are often used in pain management and neurological treatments. In contrast, multipolar IPGs utilize multiple electrodes to provide more targeted and adjustable stimulation, which can enhance therapeutic outcomes in complex conditions. These technologies cater to diverse medical needs, driving advancements and innovations in the field of neurostimulation and pain management therapies.</p></p>
