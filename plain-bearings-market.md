<p><strong>The Plain Bearings Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Radial (radial) Plain Bearing</li><li>Thrust (axial) Plain Bearing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The plain bearings market is primarily categorized into radial and thrust bearings. Radial plain bearings support loads perpendicular to the shaft axis, allowing rotational movement with minimal friction, making them ideal for applications like motors and conveyors. Thrust plain bearings handle axial loads, supporting forces parallel to the shaft, suitable for applications such as automotive clutches and gearboxes. Both types are essential in machinery, providing efficient operation and longevity in performance across various industrial sectors.</p></p>
