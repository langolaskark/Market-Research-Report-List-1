<p><strong>The Giant Magnetoresistive (GMR) Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Standard Multilayer</li><li>High Temperature Multilayer</li><li>Low Hysteresis High Temperature Multilayer</li><li>Spin Valve</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Giant Magnetoresistive (GMR) sensors are categorized based on their construction and performance characteristics. Standard multilayer sensors consist of alternating magnetic and non-magnetic layers, achieving efficient magnetic field detection. High-temperature multilayer sensors operate effectively in elevated temperatures, ideal for harsh environments. Low hysteresis high-temperature multilayer sensors minimize signal lag, enhancing precision. Spin valve sensors utilize a different mechanism involving magnetic spin alignment for sensing. The "Other" category includes innovative designs and hybrid technologies that may combine elements from the above types for specialized applications.</p></p>
