<p><p>The Analog IC Testing Machine market comprises two main types: Wafer Testers and Packaged Device Testers. Wafer Testers evaluate integrated circuits while they are still part of a silicon wafer, allowing for high throughput and early defect detection. Packaged Device Testers, on the other hand, assess finished ICs within their protective packages. This stage ensures functionality and reliability before deployment. Together, these testers support the manufacturing process by ensuring quality and performance standards in analog IC production.</p></p>
