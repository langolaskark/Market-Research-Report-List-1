<p><p>Large scale LNG terminals are categorized into various market types based on their functions and locations. </p><p>Liquefaction terminals transform natural gas into liquid form for efficient transportation. Onshore terminals are located on land, facilitating easier integration with existing infrastructure. Offshore terminals are built on platforms in the ocean, minimizing land use and environmental impact. </p><p>Regasification terminals convert LNG back into gas for distribution. Similar to liquefaction, they also exist in onshore and offshore formats, ensuring flexibility in supply chains and market access.</p></p>
