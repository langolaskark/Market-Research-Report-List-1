<p><p>The polyimide (PI) market is segmented into three primary types: aliphatic (linear polyimides), semi-aromatic, and aromatic polyimides. Aliphatic polyimides are known for their flexibility and low thermal stability, making them suitable for applications requiring high elongation. Semi-aromatic polyimides combine moderate thermal stability with good mechanical properties, ideal for various industrial uses. Aromatic polyimides exhibit exceptional thermal stability and rigidity, making them well-suited for aerospace, electronics, and high-performance applications. Each type serves distinct sectors based on performance characteristics.</p></p>
