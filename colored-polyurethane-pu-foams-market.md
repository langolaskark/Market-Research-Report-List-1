<p><p>The colored polyurethane (PU) foams market is segmented into rigid and flexible types. Rigid PU foams are primarily used for insulation in construction and refrigeration due to their low thermal conductivity and strength. They are often employed in applications requiring structural support. In contrast, flexible PU foams are more versatile, commonly found in furniture, automotive seating, and mattresses, providing comfort and cushioning. Both types can be produced in various colors to meet aesthetic and functional design requirements.</p></p>
