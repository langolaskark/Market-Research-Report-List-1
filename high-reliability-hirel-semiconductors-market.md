<p><p>The High Reliability (HiRel) Semiconductors market is categorized mainly into Surface Mount Technology (SMT) and Through Hole Technology (THT). Surface Mount Technology involves mounting components directly onto the surface of printed circuit boards, offering compact designs and enhanced production efficiency. In contrast, Through Hole Technology entails inserting component leads through holes in the PCB, providing robust connections ideal for environments demanding high durability. Both technologies cater to sensitive applications in aerospace, military, and medical sectors, where reliability is crucial.</p></p>
