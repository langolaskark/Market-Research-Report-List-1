<p><strong>The Ti Etchant Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Hydrogen Peroxide</li><li>Fluoro Polymer</li><li>Hydrofluoric Acid</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Ti etchant market is categorized into three main types: Hydrogen Peroxide, Fluoro Polymer, and Hydrofluoric Acid. Hydrogen Peroxide is known for its effectiveness in oxidizing titanium, while Fluoro Polymer is utilized for its selective etching properties and low environmental impact. Hydrofluoric Acid, a traditional etchant, offers rapid etching but requires careful handling due to its hazardous nature. These etchants are crucial in the semiconductor and manufacturing industries for patterning and surface modification of titanium substrates.</p></p>
