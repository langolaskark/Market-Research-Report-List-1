<p><p>Signal repeaters are devices that amplify and retransmit signals to enhance communication quality over distances. The market is primarily divided into two types: analog and digital. Analog signal repeaters are designed to boost continuous signals, often used in traditional communication systems like radio and television. Digital signal repeaters, on the other hand, process discrete signals, ensuring the integrity of data transmission in modern applications like cellular networks and internet services. Each type serves distinct technological requirements and applications.</p></p>
