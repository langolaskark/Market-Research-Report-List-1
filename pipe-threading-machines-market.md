<p><p>Pipe threading machines are essential tools for creating threaded ends on pipes, and the market can be segmented into pillar type and bed type machines. Pillar type machines are compact, with vertical pillars that support the threading mechanism, making them suitable for smaller workshops with limited space. In contrast, bed type machines have a horizontal design and a larger bed area, allowing for the threading of longer pipes and higher production volumes. Both types serve specific needs in various industrial applications.</p></p>
