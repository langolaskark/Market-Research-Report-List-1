<p><p>The fortified gummies market is primarily divided into two types: pectin-based and gelatin-based gummies. Pectin-based gummies utilize plant-derived pectin as a gelling agent, making them suitable for vegan consumers and those seeking natural ingredients. On the other hand, gelatin-based gummies are made from animal-derived gelatin, offering a traditional chewy texture. Both types cater to health-conscious individuals by fortifying their products with vitamins, minerals, and other beneficial nutrients, appealing to a wide range of consumers seeking convenient health supplements.</p></p>
