<p><p>The wax paper market is segmented into two main types: dry wax paper and wet wax paper. Dry wax paper is coated with a thin layer of wax, making it ideal for wrapping food items and providing moisture resistance. It is commonly used for storage and packaging. In contrast, wet wax paper features a thicker wax layer, offering enhanced moisture and grease resistance, making it suitable for baking and cooking applications. Both types cater to distinct needs in food preservation and packaging solutions.</p></p>
