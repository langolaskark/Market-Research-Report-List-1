<p><p>Light Sheet Fluorescence Microscopy (LSFM) encompasses several market types, including Single Plane Illumination Microscopy (SPIM) and Double-Illumination Selective Plane Illumination Microscopy (DiSPIM). SPIM utilizes a single light sheet to illuminate a specimen, providing fast imaging with minimal photodamage. DiSPIM, on the other hand, employs two light sheets for improved signal-to-noise ratio and enhanced imaging depth. Other market types may include variations and hybrid techniques that combine LSFM with other microscopy methods to enhance versatility and application in biological research.</p></p>
