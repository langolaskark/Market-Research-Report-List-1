Genetic biomarkers involve alterations in DNA, such as mutations or gene expressions, which help in risk assessment, early detection, and personalized therapy strategies. Both types enhance patient outcomes by facilitating targeted treatments and improving diagnostic accuracy in oncology care.</p></p>
