<p><ul><li>Hall Type</li><li>Magnetic Electric Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Truck and Bus Wheel Speed Sensor market encompasses two main types: Hall Type and Magnetic Electric Type. Hall Type sensors utilize a Hall effect phenomenon to detect wheel speed, offering high accuracy and reliability. In contrast, Magnetic Electric Type sensors rely on the magnetic field generated by the wheel's rotation to produce electrical signals. Both types are crucial for vehicle stability control, anti-lock braking systems, and overall safety, catering to the growing demand for efficient vehicle performance in commercial transport.</p></p>
