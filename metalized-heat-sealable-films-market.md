<p><strong>The Metalized Heat Sealable Films Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Biaxially Oriented Polyethylene Terephthalate (BOPET) Films</li><li>Biaxially Oriented Polypropylene (BOPP) Films</li><li>Cast Polypropylene (CPP) Films</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Metalized heat sealable films are specialized packaging materials that enhance barrier properties and visual appeal. Biaxially oriented polyethylene terephthalate (BOPET) films are known for their strength and transparency, making them suitable for high-quality graphics and moisture resistance. Biaxially oriented polypropylene (BOPP) films offer excellent clarity and are often used for food packaging due to their low sealing temperatures. Cast polypropylene (CPP) films are versatile and provide excellent heat sealability, making them ideal for flexible packaging applications.</p></p>
