<p><p>Hyper-Spectral Imaging (HSI) equipment is categorized into several types based on wavelength ranges. Visible/Near Infrared (VNIR) systems capture light from visible to near-infrared wavelengths, ideal for agriculture and environmental monitoring. Short Wave Infrared (SWIR) covers wavelengths that are useful in mineralogy and food quality inspection. Medium Wave Infrared (MWIR) is effective for thermal imaging and gas detection, while Long Wave Infrared (LWIR) is typically used for thermal surveillance and security. Other market types may include specific applications or technologies that combine these ranges for enhanced analysis.</p></p>
