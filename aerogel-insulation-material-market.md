<p><p>The aerogel insulation material market can be categorized into three main types: blanket, particle, and others. Blanket aerogels are flexible sheets that offer high thermal resistance and are used in building insulation and industrial applications. Particle aerogels, composed of small granules, are utilized for their lightweight properties and excellent thermal performance in diverse settings, including aerospace and oil and gas industries. Other types include monoliths and composite forms, serving specialized applications where unique thermal or acoustic properties are required.</p></p>
