<p><strong>The Organosol Lignins Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Ethanol/Water Pulping (Alcell Process)</li><li>Pulping With Acetic Acid (CIMV Process)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Organosol Lignins market is primarily categorized into two processes: Ethanol/Water Pulping (Alcell Process) and Pulping with Acetic Acid (CIMV Process). The Alcell Process utilizes a solvent mixture of ethanol and water to efficiently extract lignin from biomass, facilitating the production of high-purity lignin for various applications. Conversely, the CIMV Process employs acetic acid as a solvent, enabling the selective extraction of lignin while preserving cellulose. Both methods are essential for producing lignin with unique properties for industrial use.</p></p>
