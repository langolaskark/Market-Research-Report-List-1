<p><p>The Focused Ultrasound System market is primarily divided into two types: Ultrasound-Guided Focused Ultrasound Systems and MR-Guided Focused Ultrasound Systems. Ultrasound-Guided systems utilize real-time ultrasound imaging to precisely target tissue for therapeutic applications, offering benefits such as portability and cost-effectiveness. In contrast, MR-Guided systems employ magnetic resonance imaging to provide detailed soft tissue visualization, ensuring higher accuracy in targeting and monitoring treatment effects. Both types are pivotal for non-invasive procedures, enhancing treatment for various medical conditions.</p></p>
