<p><p>The Magnetic Localization System market is divided into two main types: Rigid and Flexible Magnetic Localization Systems. Rigid Magnetic Localization Systems provide stable and precise positioning using fixed magnetic fields, often utilized in a range of surgical and diagnostic applications. In contrast, Flexible Magnetic Localization Systems offer adaptability and mobility, allowing for easier adjustment during procedures and better integration with varying medical environments. Both systems enhance navigational accuracy and efficacy in clinical settings, supporting improved patient outcomes.</p></p>
